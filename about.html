What We're Building

Our Product helps SMBs to leverage power of Whatsapp to grow their business. By downloading Bikayi app, just by uploading images, they get their own website/catalog and also variety of tools to share it as Whatsapp message or status/video and track it at the same time by providing them customer metrics.
